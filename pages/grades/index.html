Grades

With reluctance, I've given into using engrade. There are things I don't like about it, but I've made some adjustments to force it to fit more closely with our class goals and structure.

If I had a million hours in the day, I would update your grades in real-time. But, I don't. To keep everything sane and predictable, I will post grade updates on the following schedule. If you come to class and do your work, you should have nothing to worry about.

February 25, 2014 Mid Term Grade Update
March 7, 2014 Second Update
March 21, 2014 Penultimate Update and failure warnings
March 28, 2014 Final Grades

Late Work Policy

All assignments are due on the due date posted. Assignments turned in one class period late will be reduced by 1 letter grade. Anything submitted after that will not be graded.

Revision Policy

Students are welcome to revise all on-time work in order to improve their grades.
